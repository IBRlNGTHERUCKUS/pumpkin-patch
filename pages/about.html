About Us

Paddington Farms is a 16 Hectare farm located in the beautiful city of Yucaipa. Proudly owned by the Paddington Family, The farm has recently been opened to the public! So stop on by for delicious food, beautiful views, and of course, the perfect pumpkin!

Our Mission

Since our founding in 1973 our goal has been to provide high quality pesticide free pumpkins to San Bernardino and it's surrounding counties

Parking

Parking is located at the end of Fairly Road off of I-75. Due to limited parking, a fee of $15 per vehicle will be charged.

FAQS

  • Do you have any vegan options?
    • Yes! We have many vegetarian and vegan beverages, as well as food items.
  • What time do you open?
    • We are open Friday-Sunday from 4PM to 10PM.
  • Do you serve alcohol?
    • Of course! We serve homebrewed pumpkin ale, lavender mead, and fall themed mixed drinks at our outdoor saloon!
